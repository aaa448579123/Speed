MySQL5.7版本sql_mode=only_full_group_by问题解决办法
1.查看sql_mode
SELECT @@sql_mode;
结果例：ONLY_FULL_GROUP_BY,STRICT_TRANS_TABLES,NO_ZERO_IN_DATE,NO_ZERO_DATE,ERROR_FOR_DIVISION_BY_ZERO
2.去掉查询结果中的ONLY_FULL_GROUP_BY
SET sql_mode = '剩余内容'
例：SET sql_mode = 'STRICT_TRANS_TABLES,NO_ZERO_IN_DATE,NO_ZERO_DATE,ERROR_FOR_DIVISION_BY_ZERO'
